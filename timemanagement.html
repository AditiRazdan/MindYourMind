Time Management
Individuals who struggle with time management are significantly more likely to feel like their lives are out of control and experience stress along with various mental and physical health issues. Being organized with your time can enhance your self-discipline and enable you to concentrate on other aspects of your life. Whether its stress from feeling overwhelmed by school, sports, or another area, adopting time management strategies can elevate your self-esteem and productivity while greatly reducing your stress levels.
Breathing Exercises
When stressed, your breathing pattern changes, and your breaths typically become shallow and faster. This disrupts the balance of gases in the body, making symptoms of stress persist and worsen. Breathing exercises can help manage stress because of their ability to calm the nervous system, allowing the brain and body to relax and slow down
